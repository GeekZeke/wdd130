Overview

Purpose

The purpose of Mana Vault is to bring information about cEDH to many Magic the Gathering players. Most websites dedicated to the format are clunky, outdated, and poor looking. Mana Vault is here to solve that problem by offering stylish and efficent information regarding to this Magic the Gathering format

Audience

The audience for Mana Vault is new and old cEDH players who are looking for current decks, tournament results, and new card releases

Content

Home page

[Visitors of ManaVault will come to the site to keep up to date on cEDH content. Whether the viewer wants to see what decks exist in each color and how they’re currently performing or seeing what kind of experimental additions and strategies are being created and tested. Visitors will also be able to see featured videos and articles from prominent members of the community and should expect to be immersed in cEDH content. To fully engage with ManaVault users will need extensive decklists, cards, and tournament results. Detailed guides on topics such as in depth explorations of strategies, interactions, decks, and meta analysis are critical. Ensuring quality is the top priority for ManaVault, maintaining decklists to be up to date and accurate. ]
